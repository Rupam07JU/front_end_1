Treatments Available On Kidney Diseases

Surgery For Kidney Stones

Common reasons for kidney removal surgery include illness, injury or to donate a kidney to a kidney transplant patient. Depending on the condition or reason, a doctor may remove part of the kidney, the entire kidney, or the kidney plus other surrounding tissues and organs.

After this, a special expandable metal tube (a ‘stent’) is usually put into the artery, expanded, and left there to keep the artery open..

Surgery To Remove Kidney Cysts

Kidney cysts are fluid-filled pouches that form in the kidneys; they can cause extreme pain or even obstruct the kidney. Surgeons can remove these through a minimally invasive procedure using a laparoscope; sometimes open surgery is necessary..

Stent Placement

Sometimes kidney stones (or a tumor) block the ureter—the tube that connects your kidney to your bladder. When this happens, a kidney surgeon may opt to place a stent..
