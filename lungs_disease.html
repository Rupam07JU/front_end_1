Treatments Available On Lungs Diseases

Empyema

Empyema is a collection of pus in the space between the lung and the inner surface of the chest wall (pleural space).

Pneumothoraxy

A collapsed lung occurs when air escapes from the lung. The air then fills the space outside of the lung between the lung and chest wall. This buildup of air puts pressure on the lung, so it cannot expand as much as it normally does when you take a breath. The medical name of this condition is pneumothorax..

Lobectomy

The lungs have sections called lobes. The right lung has 3 lobes. The left lung has 2 lobes.A lobectomy is a surgery to remove one of the lobes of the lungs.

Lung transplant

A lung transplant is a surgical procedure to replace a diseased or failing lung with a healthy lung, usually from a deceased donor.
